Researcher Profile

I received my PhD in Aerospace Engineering from the University of Cincinnati in 2023. I am currently working as a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Department of Aerospace Engineering and Engineering Mechanics. My dissertation work was on machine learning of multiphase flows, and my postdoctoral work is on fluid-structure interactions in high-speed supersonic and hypersonic flows. I also have several years if experience in industry. I am currently looking for opportunities to continue working on hypersonics, fluid structure interactions, multiphase flows, combustion and machine learning.
