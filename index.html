Welcome to my website - where I share my research activities in Aerospace Engineering. I specialize in high-fidelity, direct numerical and large eddy simulations of non-reacting and reacting flows. I also specialize in resolving flows scales and turbulence modeling, multiphase flows, combustion and machine learning.

Brief Research Preview

Hypersonic & Supersonic Flows

I work on high-fidelity numerical simulations for flows in Hypersonic regimes, up to Mach 12. The flow grids are well resolved for a coarse direct numerical simulation approach.

Fluid Structure Interactions

I am working on high fidelity numerical simulations for fluid-structure-interactions (FSI) studies in supersonic and hypersonic flows.

Multiphase Flows & Combustion

I conduct numerical simulations for studying all aspects of multiphase flows, including jet breakup, droplet breakup, atomization, etc.

Machine Learning

I build, design and develop machine learning algorithms and frameworks for aerospace applications using data from simulation and experimental studies to predict spatiotemporally varying and statistically stationary flows.
